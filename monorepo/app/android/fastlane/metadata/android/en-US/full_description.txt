PET Roll is the complete pet health management app for dedicated pet owners and professional breeders.

MULTI-PET PROFILES
Create detailed profiles for all your pets — dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, and more. Track breed, weight, age, and upload photos to build a complete health portfolio.

VACCINATION TRACKING & REMINDERS
Never miss a vaccination again. Log every vaccine, get smart reminders before they're due, and keep a verified vaccination history ready for vet visits or travel.

COMPLETE MEDICAL HISTORY
Build a comprehensive health timeline for each pet. Record vet visits, diagnoses, surgeries, medications, and treatments — all organized chronologically.

PREGNANCY TRACKING
Monitor your pet's pregnancy week by week with milestone checklists, weight tracking, and care instructions tailored to each species. Know exactly what to expect at every stage.

FEEDING & MEDICATION SCHEDULES
Set daily feeding times and medication reminders. Mark completions, track missed doses, and ensure your pet's routine is never disrupted.

MATING & BREEDING
Browse verified breeders, search for compatible mates by breed and location, send match requests, and connect directly through in-app chat.

HEALTH REPORTS
Generate professional PDF health reports with your pet's complete medical history — perfect for vet referrals, travel documentation, or breeder records.

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
Stay informed with timely reminders for vaccinations, medications, feeding times, and pregnancy milestones. Notifications work even when the app is closed.

BREEDER VERIFICATION
Apply for verified breeder status to build trust with potential matches. Verified breeders get a badge displayed on all their listings.

BUILT FOR PET LOVERS
Whether you have one family pet or manage a breeding program, PET Roll keeps all your pet health information organized, accessible, and shareable.
